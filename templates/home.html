About Chatbook

Large language models such as ChatGPT are good at answering general questions from a user. However, they are not well-suited to semantically pick out relevant text from textbooks and lengthy PDF files, nor do they make use of this information retrieval towards proven active recall methods.

That's where Chatbook comes in.





We introduce a novel Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) solution that combines information retrieval through semantic search with active recall methods including flashcard generation to create a chatbot that improves understanding of textbook content and the quality of study sessions.





Developed by Anirudh Chintaluri and Vatsal Sivaratri, TJHSST Computer Systems Research Lab 2024-25
